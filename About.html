About

Technological University,Meiktila is located on the right side of Meiktila-Pindale Road in Meiktila,Mandalay Region.Its area is 51.65 acres.The University was transferred to the present site in 2008.The former University was in Tawma Village-tract east of Meiktila.It was originally formed on 1 December 1986 as Government Technological College on 25 November 1999.The University was inaugurated don 20 January 2007 at the place.
